Welcome to Car Counting Game Mode 1

Follow the steps to learn how to play the game.

Rules

1. Count cars of specific colours as they pass by on the road, with adult supervision ensuring safety and guidance. Please stand at a safe place away from the road

2. Click on the buttons to count the cars based on their colour. Once you reach 100 Cars the game ends

3. The game will show you how many cars you clicked from the total 100 cars.

Settings

In the settings menu, you can toggle music on or of, change volume of background or effects volume.

The settings will be saved in your browser across different modes.

Current Weather is the weather at your current location if you have allowed permission for geolocation.

Developer info and social media links are also available in the settings menu.

Step 1:

Double click on the Start button to start.

Step 2:

Click on the buttons when you see a car.

Step 3:

When total count reaches 100 the game ends.

Step 4:

When the game ends, it shows you how many different cars you clicked from the total 100 car.

Click on the close button to close the game or go to home page.
