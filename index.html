Car Counting Game

Enjoy a fun and educational car counting game! Test your skills by counting cars based on colour, identifying different vehicle types, and recognising car brands. Perfect for children to enhance cognitive abilities while having fun.

Choose a Game Mode

Car Counter Mode 1

Count cars of specific colours as they pass by on the road, with adult supervision ensuring safety and guidance. The activity builds cognitive skills by challenging players to focus, observe, and distinguish between different coloured vehicles. As children engage in this game, they develop their ability to categorise and identify objects, promoting cognitive growth in a fun and interactive way.

Car Counter Mode 2

Count cars as they move across the screen, with the level increasing as the count rises. As players progress, the challenge intensifies by introducing cars of various colours. This encourages children to not only count but also identify and tally cars based on their hues. With each level presenting a mix of differently coloured cars, players sharpen their attention and colour recognition skills while having fun.
