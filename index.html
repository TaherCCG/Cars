Enjoy a fun and educational car counting game! Test your skills by counting cars based on colour, identifying different vehicle types, and recognising car brands. Perfect for children to enhance cognitive abilities while having fun.

Choose a Game Mode

Car Counter Mode 1

Count cars of various colours passing by, under adult supervision. This activity fosters cognitive skills, challenging players to focus and distinguish vehicles.

Car Counter Mode 2

Count cars moving across the screen. As players advance, speed intensifies, encouraging counting, identification, and tallying based on hues. Sharpen attention and colour recognition skills while having fun.
